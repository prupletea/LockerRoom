# LockerRoom
CS Girlies November Hackaton - Make Learning Cool Again!

## Idea
The main idea is to have a unified platform for keeping track of assignments, grades, for studying and more, while also making it feel like a game. Locker Room - because it's where you keep everything necessary to reach your academic goals! Inside your space-themed locker lives a little creature - an alien and completing each assignment or task makes your little companion happier!

## Inspirations
Habitica and the fact that I do all of my tracking on Google Sheets.
